Let's start with...

Editors

Overview

  • Educator
  • Distributor
  • Introducer
  • Time Manager
  • Orator
  • Relations Manager

General Duties

  • Be responsible for ALL printed material
    • Club Newsletter
    • Divisional Newsletter
    • The Empire Key (District Newsletter)
    • KEY CLUB Magazine (International Newsletter)
    • Fliers, bulletins, publicity pieces
  • TAKING PICTURES!!

Long Term Duties

  • Weekly
    • Attend club meetings
    • Document events attended (articles/pictures)
    • Create promotional material for events
  • Monthly
    • Attend Kiwanis-sponsored meetings
    • Work closely with Webmaster to update material
  • Save all publications to give to successor

Editor Skills

  • Organized, Flexible, Resourceful, and CREATIVE
  • Knowledge of dates and current news
    • Club projects and plans
    • LTG updates
    • District and International events
  • Knowledge of the programs and structure of Key Club International
  • Know how to use a camera (legit)

Communication

  • You must:
    • Produce a monthly publication
    • Make public service announcements (local newspapers, school intercom
    • RECRUIT new members!
  • Optional, but recommended:
    • Download free templates from keyclub.org

Publications

  • Maintain high quality publications
    • high quality photos!
  • Send articles to "higher-level" publications (Divisional, The Empire Key, KEY Club Magazine)
  • Distribute via Issuu and/or email to club members
  • Encourage members to submit articles!

What to Include

  • Cover page
  • Bulletin Editor’s greeting
  • District, Major Emphasis Projects, ELIMINATE
  • Service Spotlight
  • Club Board’s Message
  • Reminders
    • Next club meetings (location, date, time)
    • Upcoming Events
  • Officer contact information

More Ideas

  • Key Clubber of the Month – recognize hardest working or distinguished Key Clubber
  • Games or puzzles
  • Fun facts (about the board?)
  • Graphics!

Programs to Use

  • Word (Publisher for extra fanciness)
  • Excel
  • Powerpoint
  • Photoshop
* Note: these are suggested; by no means required

Remember to export as PDF before sending it!

Webmasters

What is a webmaster?

According to Google, webmasters have garnered much popularity in the recent decade.
  • Website Manager
  • Excited about the job
  • Builder

  • Someone who knows how to use technology to his/her advantage

General Duties

  • Maintain a website
  • Update the website.
  • Update it!
  • Make sure the website is updated!
  • And most importantly, STAY ACTIVE

Daily/Weekly Duties

  • Daily
    • Make sure the website works
    • Help with any technological problem
    • PROMOTE YOUR CLUB
  • Weekly
    • Add club meeting agendas
    • Put up events for members
    • Update the website with news
    • Upload pictures!

Long Term Duties

  • Monthly
    • Attend club meetings
    • Update members on website status
    • Ask for feedback
  • Annually
    • Include contact information for officers
    • Have updated documents (club resources)
    • Make sure that the website is in tip-top shape
    • MAKE SURE THE DOMAIN IS PAID

Skills

  • Coding:  HTML, CSS, Javascript
  • Word
  • Photoshop
  • Optional:
    • Text Editor (Vim, Emacs, Sublime, Notepad++)
    • Adobe Dreamweaver
    • Microsoft Visual Studio

Finding a Host

There are many free ones out there!
  • github.com (if you prefer coding)
  • weebly.com
  • wix.com
  • wordpress.com
  • webs.com
  • sites.google.com

What to Include

  • About Key Club
  • Cabinet and contact information
  • Club news/updates
  • Club events
  • Resources
  • Hours/points

Webmaster Notes

  • Follow the brand guide!
  • Try making your own graphics!
  • Make the website clean and easy to navigate
  • Visually pleasing websites bring members back!
  • You are a role model for the members!
